ERS Basics
The Expense Reimbursement System (ERS)

The ERS manages the process of reimbursing company employees for expenses incurred while on company time.

All employees have the option to submit requests for reimbursement and view their past tickets and pending requests. Finance managers approve and deny requests for expense reimbursement.
